About Our Organization

Global potentials Exploring Network is a non-governmental, non-profit organisation registered under law in Nigeria to transforms the lives of youth from underserved communities through entrepreneurship skills acquisition and education, service learning and agriculture into globally competitive leaders of positive change in their lives and communities thereby creating a peaceful environment for all.

At GPEN it’s our motivation to see that people are helped, motivated, supported, promoted and placed on a procreative platform for the betterment of their lives, that of their families their communities and make an extension of such noble gestures towards national development

We exists to invest in the next generation of Nigerians, providing young people with the tools they need to break the poverty cycle and create a sufficient and sustainable life for themselves and their families. We do this through a range of primary to post-secondary programs for boys and girls and work with schools and their communities across Nigeria, focussing on three important areas: Education, Entrepreneurship and SKill aquisition.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a global link that will promote peace, help in addressing health and agricultural issues, support people in becoming useful and self-reliant through skills acquisition and entrepreneurship to alleviate poverty and facilitate through various means and the development of a dynamic and information world.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be outstanding in delivery of humanitarian aid to all, providing safe space and building lives. Linking people to opportunities that will enable them become independent.
